Here is a sample application for leave:

[Your Name]
[Your Address]
[City, State, Pincode]
[Date]

[Recipient's Name]
[Recipient's Designation]
[Institution/Organization]
[Institution/Organization Address]
[City, State, Pincode]

Subject: Application for Leave

Dear [Recipient's Name],

I am writing to request a leave of [number] days from [start date] to [end date]. I have a [briefly mention the reason for leave, e.g. personal reason, family function, medical treatment, etc.].

I will make sure that all my tasks are completed and my work is up to date before taking the leave. I will also be available by email if anything urgent comes up while I am away.

I would be grateful if you could approve my leave application. If there is any issue, please let me know and I will do my best to resolve it.

Thank you for your understanding and support.

Sincerely,

[Your Signature]
[Your Name]

Note: You can modify the application as per your needs and requirements. Also, make sure to include your correct details and the reason for leave.
